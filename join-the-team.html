Build Your Career With TCS Real Estate International

We are building something meaningful. If you are passionate about accessible real estate and want to be part of a mission-driven team, we want to hear from you.

For Licensed Real Estate Agents

Join the TCS Real Estate International Team

The most impactful way to be part of TCS Real Estate International is to join as a licensed agent under Toni's national team and our affiliated brokerage. This is more than a brokerage move. It is joining a mission.

As a team agent, you will have the backing, the tools, and the community to build a business you are proud of, serving clients who need agents who truly understand them.

What Team Agents Receive

  • Full TCS Real Estate International membership included at no additional cost
  • Direct mentorship from Toni Schafer
  • Access to all weekly coaching calls, ASL interpreted
  • Full course library including Deaf Friendly Certification
  • Private community of mission-aligned agents
  • Business templates, systems, and tools

How It Works

1
Apply using the form below. Tell us about yourself, your goals, and why TCS Real Estate International is the right fit.
2
Discovery Call with Toni to discuss your goals, the team structure, and whether this is the right fit for both of us.
3
Onboard with the team. Get access to your membership, your community, and everything you need to get started.

Real Estate Educators

Licensed agents and brokers who want to contribute to curriculum development and mentorship within the TCS Real Estate International platform.
