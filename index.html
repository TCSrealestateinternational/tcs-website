Meet Toni

A Realtor Who Speaks Your Language

Toni Schafer is a licensed Realtor, ASL-fluent educator, and the founder of TCS Real Estate International. She saw firsthand how the Deaf community was underserved in real estate and decided to change that.

Today, she trains agents across the country to build thriving, accessible businesses. She is also actively training Deaf individuals to earn their real estate licenses, pass their exams, and build wealth through homeownership and real estate careers.

"The Deaf community deserves agents who understand their world, not just their words."
