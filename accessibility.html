Our Commitment

Accessibility for Everyone

TCS Real Estate International was built with accessibility at its core. We believe every person, regardless of disability or communication style, deserves equal access to real estate education and resources.

Our Standards

Built to WCAG 2.1 AA

This website is designed and maintained to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Our commitment includes:

  • Semantic HTML5 structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Keyboard navigation throughout the entire site
  • Descriptive alt text on all images
  • Sufficient color contrast on all text and interface elements
  • ARIA labels and roles for interactive components
  • Skip navigation link on every page
  • Visible focus indicators for keyboard users
  • Responsive design that works at any text size
  • Support for reduced-motion preferences
What We Provide

Features That Support Your Access

These are specific accommodations and design choices built into TCS Real Estate International's website and programs.

ASL-First Content

Our courses and community are built with American Sign Language users in mind. Videos include visual demonstrations and ASL explanations wherever possible.

Screen Reader Compatible

All pages use semantic markup with ARIA roles, landmarks, and labels so screen readers can accurately convey page structure and interactive elements.

Keyboard Navigation

Every function, form, and navigation element on this website is operable by keyboard alone. Tab order is logical and focus is always visible.

High Contrast Design

Text and background color combinations throughout the site meet or exceed WCAG AA contrast ratios, supporting users with low vision or color vision differences.

Plain Language

Content is written in clear, straightforward language. We avoid jargon and write at a reading level that is accessible to a broad audience.

Multiple Contact Methods

You can reach us by email, through the contact form, or via social media. We will work with you to find the communication method that works best for you.

Transparency

Known Limitations

While we work hard to meet accessibility standards across the entire site, some areas may fall short. Known limitations include:

  • Third-party forms: The registration and membership forms are embedded from GoHighLevel. While we have configured them for accessibility, some behaviors are controlled by the third-party provider and may not be fully compliant.
  • Older PDF documents: Any downloadable documents or guides may not be fully screen reader accessible. Please contact us if you need an accessible version of any document.
  • Video captions: Videos hosted on external platforms rely on those platforms for captioning. We strive to ensure all videos have accurate captions, but some user-generated or older content may have gaps.

We are actively working to address these limitations. If you discover an issue not listed here, please let us know using the feedback form below.
